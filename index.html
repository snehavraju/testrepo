National Institute of Technology, Karnataka (NITK),
formerly known as Karnataka Regional Engineering
College (KREC), also known as NIT Surathkal,
is a public engineering university at Surathkal,
Mangalore. It was founded in 1960 as KREC
while today, it is one of the 31 National Institutes
of Technology in India and is recognised as an
Institute of National Importance by the
Government of India.
